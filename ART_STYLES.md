# Art Styles

This document defines the art styles supported by Character Forge and their constraints.

---

## Supported Styles (MVP)

### Anime (Primary)
- Clean lineart
- Cel shading
- Stylized anatomy
- High silhouette consistency

Substyles (future):
- Shonen
- Fantasy anime
- Chibi
- Soft anime

---

## Planned Styles (Post-MVP)

### Cartoon 2D
- Simplified shapes
- Bold outlines
- Flat colors

### Flat Illustration
- Minimal shading
- Vector-friendly
- UI-focused visuals

---

## Experimental Styles

### Semi-Realistic Illustration
- Painterly shading
- Fantasy concept art
- Requires strict template locking

### Pixel Art
- Grid-aligned assets
- Limited resolution
- Strong style enforcement required

---

## Explicitly Unsupported
- Photorealism
- 3D / CGI
- Animation-based styles
